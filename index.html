Instructions

Dastardly circles are attacking your home, stop them by building towers that'll shoot them down. Bunkers can be placed on the grass, Battleships can be placed on the lakes, and Mountain Perches can be placed on the mountains. You gain gold to buy more towers by taking down circles. When your lives reach 0 you lose. Press start wave when your ready for the next attack from the circles. Now go protect you family and livelihood and press new game.
